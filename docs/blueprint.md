# **App Name**: Ramesh Jewellers Online

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display a carousel of featured jewelry and collections to captivate visitors immediately.
- Services Overview: Detail Ramesh Jewellers' services, such as custom design, repairs, appraisals, and cleaning, highlighting expertise and customer care.
- Contact Form and Information: A contact form is present with all the other useful contact info such as physical address and telephone number

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep gold (#D4AF37) to evoke luxury and tradition.
- Background color: Soft off-white (#F8F8FF) to allow jewelry to stand out.
- Accent color: Burgundy (#800020) to complement the gold with sophistication.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for elegance and high-end feel. Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability.
- Use simple, elegant line icons in gold or burgundy to represent different jewelry types or services.
- Employ a clean, minimalist layout with plenty of white space to focus attention on the jewelry images. Prioritize high-quality photography.
- Incorporate subtle animations, such as fade-ins for images or smooth scrolling effects, to enhance the user experience without distracting from the products.
